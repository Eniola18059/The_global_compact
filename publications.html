Document Registry

Publications &
Documents

The official registry of foundational instruments, governance documents, annexes, and reference materials of the Global Compact for Peace, Human Security and Responsible International Cooperation.

Core Foundational Documents

Primary Institutional
Instruments

The principal documents establishing the identity, governance, and foundational philosophy of the Global Compact.

GC/FOUND/2026
Founding Declaration

The official statement establishing the Global Compact's vision, purpose, and foundational aspirations for peace and cooperation.

GC/CHARTER/2026
Charter of Principles & Foundational Values

Fifteen articles establishing the ethical and philosophical foundation guiding all Global Compact activities and partnerships.

GC/DOCTRINE/2026
Doctrine of Divine Diplomacy

The Constitutional Morality of Global Order — the ethical framework guiding the Compact's values and institutional conduct.

GC/GOV-CONST/2026
Institutional Governance Constitution

The constitutional governance framework establishing institutional architecture, coordination mechanisms, and operational principles.

GC/MCC/2026/001
Master Consolidated Constitutional Compendium

The integrated and authoritative compilation of the Global Compact's foundational identity, principles, and institutional positioning.

GC/UDK/2026/001
UN System & Heads of State Diplomatic Dispatch Kit

The standardized diplomatic transmission protocol for circulation of Global Compact materials to international institutions.

How Documents Are
Referenced & Cited

GC / TYPE / YEAR / NUMBER
Example: GC/BRF/2026/001 — Briefing document, 2026, first issuance

Each document is uniquely traceable in the master registry. This consistent referencing system ensures institutional coherence and supports diplomatic archival review across all Global Compact publications.
